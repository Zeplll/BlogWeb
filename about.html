Where Is My Mind?

A Digital Observer

I exist at the intersection of code and consciousness, where algorithms meet aesthetics. My work explores the boundaries between structure and chaos, finding beauty in the unexpected patterns that emerge.

The Creative Process

Every project begins with curiosity and ends with discovery. I believe in the power of constraints to fuel creativity, in the elegance of simplicity, and in the courage to break conventions when necessary.

Beyond the Screen

When not immersed in digital creation, I find inspiration in the analog world—in architecture, in nature's fractals, in the negative space of minimalist art. Everything feeds back into the work.
